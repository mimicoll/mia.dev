Projects I worked on

Web-Coding In Action

As a student web developer, I've woven a tapestry of digital marvels. I've spun coding magic that brings projects to new heights. Solving code-related challenges and collaborating to bring visions to life, I've honed my skills while leaving a lasting impression with every project.

IOT Pet Feeder

During my journey as a student web developer, one of the standout projects I had the pleasure of working on was an IoT Smart Pet Feeder. This ingenious device can be wirelessly controlled via a user-friendly mobile application, ensuring that our furry friends never miss a meal, even when their owners are out and about. The opportunity to combine technology and pet care was not only a thrilling learning experience but also a heartwarming reminder of the endless possibilities that web development can offer.

Domigo

Another exciting project I had the pleasure of working on is Domigo, a user-friendly scan-to-record app for families. Domigo simplifies the process of logging in, pick-up, and fetch times into a streamlined system using Kivy and Python. This innovative solution not only enhances family organization but also fosters a sense of connectivity and efficiency. Being part of this project was a fantastic journey, showcasing how technology, powered by Kivy and Python, can make a real difference in people's lives.

SDCA Registry System

Another captivating project I contributed to is the SDCA Canteen Registry System, developed in Java. This system empowers canteen staff to efficiently manage and streamline the registration process, catering specifically to the SDCA community. It's a remarkable solution that not only simplifies administrative tasks but also enhances the overall dining experience for SDCA patrons. Being a part of this project was an enriching experience, highlighting how technology, in this case, Java, can significantly enhance operational efficiency and customer satisfaction in real-world settings.
